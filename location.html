Amsterdam

Why visit Amsterdam? Other then its world famous coffee shops, and amaing night life. Amsterdam has so much more to offer, it's amazing museums like madame tussauds, the medievil tourture museum and the world famous ann frank museum. Amsterdam also has some of the most amazing building you can find, from its narrow houses and the house that's leaning to one side. All the streets are lit up beutifully at night its an honestly an amazing city break.

Rome

Why visit Rome? Rome has some of the most beutiful buildings and monuments in the world, with the colloseum, the pantheon, and the arch of constantine to name a few. With its wrold famous cuisine your never far away from amazing food. The people in Rome are very friendly and with it been a relatively small city, you could see most of what Rome has to offer in just a couple of days. A perfect weekend get away.

Bali

Why visit Bali? Also known as the land of the gods, Bali is known for been a surfers paradise. Bali has stunning beaches, lush grenn mountains, scenic lakes, sacred river, gorgous waterfalls, amazing canyons hidden away and magical tempales. Bali is a stunning beautiful place for all people to enjoy.

I'd say its beauty, history, beaches, cenotes, marine life, and shopping are just a few more reasons to visit Mexico this year

Barbados

Why visit Barbados? Barbados is considered to be one of the safest islands in the Caribbean. People here are called Bajans; they are warm, friendly and known for their kind hospitality. They have a great sense of fun and love to dance and sing. The famous singer Rihanna is one of their shining stars and is often seen on the island
